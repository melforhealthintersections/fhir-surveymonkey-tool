Fhir-surveymonkey-tool

Tool for Converting Survey Monkey Surveys

Welcome to the Survey Monkey FHIR Tool.

Welcome to the tool used for converting Survey Monkey Suverys into FHIR Questionaires.This tool allows previous surveys created on each user's own Survey Monkey account to be converted into FHIR Questionaires.

Steps

There are a few steps required to complete this process:

1. Login to your survey monkey account:

1. The tool will ask for your personal Survey Monkey login, so that you can access the survey of your choice.

2. Pick the survey you wish to convert:

2. Pick the survey that you desire, and confirm your choice.

Completed!

The Survey will now become a FHIR Questionaire.
